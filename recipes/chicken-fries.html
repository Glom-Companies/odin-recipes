Chicken Fries Recipe

Description

Indulge in a modern twist on comfort food with our Chicken Fries recipe. Tender chicken pieces are marinated in a savory blend of herbs and spices, lightly battered, and fried to golden perfection. The result is a delightful contrast of a crisp, flavorful exterior with a juicy, tender interior that promises a satisfying crunch in every bite.

Accompanied by perfectly seasoned fries that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, this dish offers a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. The fries, sprinkled with just the right amount of salt and a hint of aromatic herbs, complement the rich, zesty chicken, making each forkful an irresistible experience. Ingredients

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 1 cup buttermilk (for marinating)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Vegetable oil, for deep frying
  • 3-4 medium Russet potatoes, cut into fries
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp dried rosemary or thyme

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Prepare the Coating
  3. Heat the Oil
  4. Coat and Fry the Chicken
  5. Prepare the Seasoned Fries
  6. Bake the Fries
  7. Assemble and Serve
